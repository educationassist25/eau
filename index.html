About Us

Education Assist for USA is your trusted partner in achieving your American educational goals, specializing in guiding international students through the graduate school application process for Master’s and Ph.D. programs in the Biological Sciences. We provide personalized support at every step-from selecting the right universities to writing and refining your Curriculum Vitae (CV), Statement of Purpose (SOP), Cover Letter, and Letters of Recommendation (LOR). We also help you prepare for interviews and navigate visa requirements. In addition, you can schedule one-on-one video consultations with experienced U.S. university faculty, ensuring you receive expert, transparent advice tailored to your unique academic journey. Our student-focused approach empowers you to submit the strongest possible application and confidently pursue your academic and professional aspirations-building a clear and successful pathway to your future in the Biological Sciences.
