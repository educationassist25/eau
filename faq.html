Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What services does Education Assist for USA provide?
We offer guidance on university selection, SOP (Statement of Purpose) and resume/essay reviews, visa documentation, interview preparation, publication assistance, and personalized counseling sessions for graduate school applications.
2. Who can benefit from your services?
Our services are ideal for students applying to Master’s and Ph.D. programs in Biological Sciences who want expert, step-by-step support to successfully navigate the U.S. admissions process.
3. How does the Initial Counseling Session work?
The 30-minute consultation allows us to review your academic profile, career goals, and build a personalized strategy for your U.S. university applications.
4. Can you help with writing and editing my Statement of Purpose (SOP)?
Yes. We provide SOP writing tailored to each university and editing services with revisions to ensure your SOP is polished, personalized, and competitive.
5. Do you provide support for resumes and CVs?
Absolutely. We offer professional formatting and content improvement to make your resume or CV stand out for graduate applications.
6. How do you help with visa and interview preparation?
We conduct mock interviews, provide personalized feedback, and guide you through documentation to ensure you feel confident and well-prepared.
7. What makes Education Assist for USA different from other consultancies?
We are guided by U.S.-based university faculty with insider knowledge, proven expertise, and a strong track record of success, offering step-by-step guidance throughout the process.
8. What is included in the Full Application Package?
The package includes SOP writing, resume/CV review, cover letter support, application guidance for multiple universities, and ongoing assistance until submission.
9. Do you help convert dissertations into publishable manuscripts?
Yes. We assist in transforming dissertations into professional, journal-ready manuscripts through editing, structuring, and formatting.
10. What is the difference between manuscript drafting and manuscript finalization?
Drafting: We create a structured manuscript from your dissertation.
Finalization: We refine, edit, and format an already-prepared manuscript for journal submission.
11. Do you provide support for writing review articles?
Yes. We prepare comprehensive review articles, including literature synthesis, critical analysis, and structured writing suitable for peer-reviewed journals.
12. Will my publication be journal-ready after your service?
Our goal is to prepare manuscripts that meet international journal standards. Final acceptance, however, depends on the journal’s scope, editorial board, and peer review process.
13. Can you help me choose the right journal for my manuscript?
Yes. We guide you in selecting suitable journals based on your research field, target audience, and desired impact.
14. Do you provide plagiarism checking and editing for publications?
Yes. We check manuscripts for plagiarism, ensure originality, and edit content to meet academic integrity and journal requirements.
15. Can you assist with revisions after reviewer feedback?
Yes. We offer support in addressing reviewer comments, making necessary revisions, and preparing resubmissions to improve the chances of acceptance.
